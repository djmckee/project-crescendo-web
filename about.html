FAQ

What is Sonata?

Sonata is an Android app designed to teach you how to compose music through a simple and easy to use musical programming interface - no sheet music knowledge required!

Sonata includes a range of step-by-step interactive tutorials, guiding the user through how to use the application and then how to write their own compositions. Compositions can be converted into sheet music, or played back as sound. They can also be exported as an industry standard MusicXML file, which can be uploaded to our hosted API, and shared via social media including Facebook & Twitter

Who created Sonata?

Sonata was created by a team of eight Newcastle University students as a submission for the module found here. Our objective was to create an app to help a user learn something.

Is this app available for iOS or Windows devices?

Unfortunately, due to time restraints only an Android version was constructed. We do not plan on releasing an alternative port of the application, nor do we intend to update the current version.
